UW Lean Hackathon 2026

  • A collaboration between the Math AI Lab and the PLSE Lab at the University of Washington with support from Amazon.

A two-day hackathon bringing together students, faculty, and industry partners to work on challenge problems showcasing the power of the Lean Proof Assistant. Each team will include at least one Lean-proficient researcher and one domain expert.
