UW Lean Hackathon 2026

The UW Math AI Lab, in conjunction with PLSE, ECE and CSE is pleased to announce a research-oriented hackathon dedicated to the advancement of the Lean Proof Assistant, taking place May 8th-10th, 2026. Participants will be organized into teams of 4-5 members to tackle ambitious challenge problems in formal verification, machine learning, and metaprogramming in Lean. With generous support from Amazon, we hope to produce functional prototypes/contributions to the Lean ecosystem while strengthening institutional partnerships between the Math AI Lab and industry.

Click here to apply to participate! The application deadline is April 17th, 2026.
